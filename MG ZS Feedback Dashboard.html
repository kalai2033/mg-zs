Overall Satisfaction

68% of owners would purchase the MG ZS again, with an average reliability rating of 4.3/5 stars. This indicates strong overall satisfaction with the vehicle.

Driving Experience

Average driving experience rating is 4.4/5. Most owners appreciate the comfort and handling, though some mention issues with engine revving on hills.

Geographic Distribution

UK and Spain have the most responses (15 and 14 respectively), followed by Italy (9) and Greece (5). Feedback is consistent across all regions.

Common Concerns

The most frequently mentioned issue is over-revving (especially on hills/highways) when the battery charges. This is normal hybrid behavior but some find it concerning.

Fuel Efficiency

Average fuel consumption is around 5.4 L/100km (typical for this hybrid model). City driving generally shows better efficiency than highway.

Ownership Duration

Average ownership period is 5.4 months (many recent purchases). Long-term owners (10+ months) consistently rate the vehicle highly (4-5 stars).

Fuel Consumption by Driving Style

Mixed driving (most common): 5.53 L/100km. City driving averages 5.81 L/100km. Highway driving most efficient at 5.60 L/100km. Hybrid efficiency depends on driving patterns.

Primary Concern: Over-Revving

18% of owners report over-revving (4-5.5k RPM) on hills and highways. This is normal hybrid battery charging behavior but causes concern due to engine noise. Software updates have reduced this issue.
